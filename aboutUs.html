Who We Are

ABOUT US

DECOTECH Metal Profile Factory An Independent Company Established in 2010 with a vision to provide high-quality building materials and products to the growing construction industry in Qatar. Over the years, we have grown into a key player in the market, known for our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Decotech Metal Profile Factory is dedicated to the production and supply of metal profiles used in the construction and decorative sectors. With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, advanced technology, and a team of skilled professionals, we deliver solutions that meet the highest standards of quality, durability, and design. And we are proud to say that it is ‘Made in Qatar’.

We specialize in creating a wide range of metal profiles, ensuring that your building projects receive the finest materials. From steel to aluminum, our products are designed to meet your exact specifications and enhance the aesthetic appeal of your spaces.

OUR MISSION

To support our customers’ construction and design projects by offering premium products, superior service, and unmatched reliability.

If you need building material for your projects we are here for you!
