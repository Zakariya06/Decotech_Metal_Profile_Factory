Our Services

Decotech Metal Profile Factory offers comprehensive services to support your building and construction projects, from consultation to delivery. We work closely with our clients to ensure that each project receives the right materials, custom profiles, and expert advice.

Here are the services we provide

Custom Metal Profile Design

  • ⦁ We collaborate with clients to create unique metal profiles for specialized projects.
  • ⦁ Our engineers and designers will work closely with your team to develop custom shapes and sizes that meet your design vision.

Fabrication Services

  • ⦁ Precision cutting, bending, and shaping of metal profiles
  • ⦁ In-house fabrication of custom profiles to suit architectural and structural requirements.

Delivery & Logistics

  • ⦁ We ensure timely delivery of our products to your project site.
  • ⦁ Efficient and reliable logistics that cover all areas within Qatar and beyond.

Consultation & Support

  • ⦁ Expert advice and technical support for product selection and application
  • ⦁ Assistance with meeting local building codes and project requirements.
