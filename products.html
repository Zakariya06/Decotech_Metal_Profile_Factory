Access Panels

Decotech Metal Profile Factory supplies and manufactures premium quality access panels for ceiling solutions, offering a wide variety of options to accommodate all ceiling types. Our ceiling access panels are engineered to provide an effective and reliable solution, meeting all customer needs. They are specifically designed to ensure easy access to voids and cavities where services, piping, and controls need to be accessible for maintenance or emergency operations. The frame is crafted from durable, powder-coated aluminum alloy (6063) for added strength and longevity.

At Decotech, we offer two main types of access panels, depending on the cladding used: Gypsum and Strip access panels. For the gypsum-based panels, we offer four different variants (Regular, Fire-rated, Moisture-resistant, and Fire & Moisture-resistant), depending on the specific gypsum requirement.

Our ceiling access panels come in both square and rectangular shapes, with seven standard sizes available. Customized sizes can also be made upon request. The selection of the type and size of the access panel is determined by the customer’s specifications and the contractor's requirements, based on the project needs or client preferences. All Decotech ceiling access panels are manufactured to the highest industry standards and come complete with all necessary hanging accessories to ensure both structural integrity and aesthetic appeal in any ceiling design.

Decotech Furring System

Decotech Furring System offers an efficient and reliable solution for creating concealed gypsum ceilings. The system consists of screw-fixed plasterboard, which is securely attached to the Ceiling Furring System. This system is suspended from joists or concrete ceilings, providing structural support for the plasterboard while maintaining a clean, seamless ceiling design.

Designed for easy installation and long-lasting performance, the Decotech Furring System is an ideal choice for projects requiring a high-quality, concealed ceiling solution. Whether for commercial or residential use, this system ensures optimal strength, flexibility, and aesthetic integration.

Decotech’s DUD-DCD suspension system

The Decotech DUD-DCD Suspended Ceiling System with metal grid provides a robust and flexible solution for installing suspended ceilings. This system consists of Decotech DCD and Decotech DUD channels. The DCD channels are securely attached to the ceiling slab using hooked wires, rapid hangers, or heavy hangers, ensuring stability and strength. These channels are then connected using clips to create a solid grid structure that supports the ceiling panels.

Designed for both commercial and residential applications, the Decotech DUD-DCD Suspended Ceiling System offers excellent flexibility, strength, and ease of installation. The system supports a wide range of ceiling finishes, making it an ideal choice for projects where both durability and aesthetic appeal are important.

Dry Wall Partition System

Our drywall partition systems provide flexible, durable, and cost-effective solutions for interior wall applications, offering sound insulation, fire resistance, and aesthetics. Drywall partition systems are increasingly popular in Qatar's expanding real estate market, particularly for offices, hotels, and residential complexes. These systems offer flexibility, quick installation, and cost-efficiency.

  • Stud & Track Systems – Essential framework components for partition construction.
  • Soundproofing Drywall Solutions – Ideal for offices, hotels, and residential spaces requiring noise reduction
  • Partition Boards – High-quality gypsum or cement boards for wall assembly.
  • Finishing Accessories – Corner beads, joint compounds, and tape for a flawless finish

Clip in metal celing tiles

Our clip-in ceiling tiles are designed for easy installation and a clean aesthetic, offering a wide range of design options. Clip-in metal ceiling tiles are frequently used in high-traffic areas like shopping malls, office buildings, and airports in Qatar due to their durability and aesthetic appeal.

  • Standard Clip-in Tiles – Ideal for commercial spaces such as offices, hotels, and restaurants in Doha and beyond.
  • Acoustic Ceiling Tiles – Widely used in corporate offices and meeting rooms to reduce noise pollution, improving sound quality in Qatar's urban settings.
  • Decorative Metal Tiles – Available in various textures and finishes, these tiles are used in prestigious developments, including luxury hotels and malls.
  • Fire-Rated Ceiling Tiles – Fire-resistant solutions that comply with the building regulations in Qatar, ensuring safety in public and high-occupancy spaces.
