Our Products

At Decotech Metal Profile Factory, we offer an extensive range of high-quality metal profiles tailored to meet the demands of construction and decorative projects. Our products are crafted with precision, ensuring durability, functionality, and style for a variety of applications.

Access Panels

Our access panels are designed for easy and convenient access to hidden or hard-to-reach areas, ideal for installation in walls, ceilings, and floors. They are essential for providing safe, easy, and convenient access to hidden areas within buildings. They are commonly used in Qatar's high-rise buildings, commercial properties, and residential developments.

  • Flush Mount Panels – Seamless integration with wall and ceiling finishes.
  • Fire Rated Access Panels – High-performance panels designed to withstand high temperatures.
  • Lockable Access Panels – Secure access solutions.
  • Non-fire Rated Panels – Cost-effective options for general access needs.

Decotech Furring System

Our furring system is a versatile solution for supporting drywall, ceiling grids, and other suspended structures. It ensures a smooth, stable base for various types of installations. Our furring systems are integral to ceiling and wall installations in both residential and commercial buildings across Qatar, offering stable frameworks for drywall and suspended ceilings.

  • Galvanized Steel Furring Channels – Corrosion-resistant and strong for long-lasting support.
  • Suspended Ceiling Channels – Designed specifically for ceiling installations.
  • Furring Strips and Hat Channels – For enhanced stability and ease of installation.
  • Fire-rated Furring Solutions – Furring systems with fire resistance to meet safety standards.

Block Work And Plastering Accessories

Our block work and plastering accessories are essential for constructing durable walls and providing professional finishes in both residential and commercial projects across Qatar.

  • Corner Beads – For smooth and neat corners in plastered walls.
  • Plaster Stop Beads – To achieve a clean, professional finish along the edge of plaster.
  • Wall Plugs & Fixings – Reliable anchoring solutions for block work and plastering.
  • Render Beads – To reinforce and provide a sharp edge for plaster applications.

Dry Wall Partition System

Our drywall partition systems provide flexible, durable, and cost-effective solutions for interior wall applications, offering sound insulation, fire resistance, and aesthetics. Drywall partition systems are increasingly popular in Qatar's expanding real estate market, particularly for offices, hotels, and residential complexes. These systems offer flexibility, quick installation, and cost-efficiency.

  • Stud & Track Systems – Essential framework components for partition construction.
  • Soundproofing Drywall Solutions – Ideal for offices, hotels, and residential spaces requiring noise reduction
  • Partition Boards – High-quality gypsum or cement boards for wall assembly.
  • Finishing Accessories – Corner beads, joint compounds, and tape for a flawless finish

Clip in metal celing tiles

Our clip-in ceiling tiles are designed for easy installation and a clean aesthetic, offering a wide range of design options. Clip-in metal ceiling tiles are frequently used in high-traffic areas like shopping malls, office buildings, and airports in Qatar due to their durability and aesthetic appeal.

  • Standard Clip-in Tiles – Ideal for commercial spaces such as offices, hotels, and restaurants in Doha and beyond.
  • Acoustic Ceiling Tiles – Widely used in corporate offices and meeting rooms to reduce noise pollution, improving sound quality in Qatar's urban settings.
  • Decorative Metal Tiles – Available in various textures and finishes, these tiles are used in prestigious developments, including luxury hotels and malls.
  • Fire-Rated Ceiling Tiles – Fire-resistant solutions that comply with the building regulations in Qatar, ensuring safety in public and high-occupancy spaces.

Furing channel

Our furring channels are used in combination with other metal framing components to support drywall and ceiling systems.

  • Standard Furring Angles – Used for framing drywall in both residential and commercial buildings.
  • Pre-galvanized Furring channels – Resistant to Qatar's humid environment, ensuring longevity and stability.
  • Adjustable Furring channels – Perfect for custom installations where precise measurements and adjustments are required.
