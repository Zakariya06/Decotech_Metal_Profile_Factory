    Access Panels

    • Flush Mount Panels – Seamless integration with wall and ceiling finishes.
    • Fire Rated Access Panels – High-performance panels designed to withstand high temperatures.
    • Lockable Access Panels – Secure access solutions.
    • Non-fire Rated Panels – Cost-effective options for general access needs.

    Decotech Furring System

Our furring systems are integral to ceiling and wall installations in both residential and commercial buildings across Qatar, offering stable frameworks for drywall and suspended ceilings.

    • Galvanized Steel Furring Channels – Corrosion-resistant and strong for long-lasting support.
    • Suspended Ceiling Channels – Designed specifically for ceiling installations.
    • Furring Strips and Hat Channels – For enhanced stability and ease of installation.
    • Fire-rated Furring Solutions – Furring systems with fire resistance to meet safety standards.
